About Us

Built in Amman, for the people who love it.

Kama Properties is a boutique development house founded on one belief: the finest homes come from developers who build few and care much.

Our Story

Why Kama exists.

Amman's luxury market is full of promises. What it needs is proof. We founded Kama Properties to develop villas the way they should be developed — honest materials, disciplined engineering, and a finish that holds up to a buyer's closest inspection.

Our first development, Hima (حِمى), is deliberately small, and its setting was chosen the same way — a quiet edge of Amman overlooking a forest of trees. Building few lets us stand behind every square metre, and lets you buy from people who know their own buildings by heart.

What We Stand For

Three commitments, kept on every project.

01

Craftsmanship

Local limestone, proven contractors, and specifications we would accept in our own homes. Quality is inspected, not assumed — at every stage of the build.

02

Transparency

Clear pricing, clear timelines, clear paperwork. You will always know exactly what you are buying, what it includes, and when it will be ready.

03

Care After Keys

Our relationship doesn't end at handover. We stand behind our villas after you move in, because our name is on the building long after the sale.
