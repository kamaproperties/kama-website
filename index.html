Hima · حمى

A protected place. By name, and by promise.

Hima is an old Arabic word for a sanctuary of nature — land kept green and untouched. It is the name of our first development, and the standard behind it: few homes, built properly, in a place worth protecting.

The Setting

Far, but close.

Hima stands in Al-Hummar, one of Amman's most distinguished addresses — yet the compound looks out over a forest of trees: mornings of birdsong and green horizons, evenings under quiet skies. The feel of a retreat, with the whole city within easy reach. Photographs don't do the view justice; this is a place you have to stand in.
